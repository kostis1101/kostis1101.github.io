Over the year I have worked on various different project of different sizes. Here is a list of some of those (with not particular order):

  • Green Elephant
    When I was younger I was kind of obsessed with complex numbers, so I through of creating some sort of library that would implement complex numbers and the respective functions. At first I was planing on implementing only very basic complex arithmetic, but I ended up creating much more. The only language I new that the time was C# (and not well at all), so that's what I worked with.
    Working on the project helped me understand complex number and there behaviour much better and allowed me to build a strong intuition, as I was actively trying to understand how complex numbers work from an algorithmic point of view (e.g. what does taking a logarithm do to a complex number and how do I tell the computer to compute that?).
    You can find the result on github.

  • United Squares
    In 2018 I got into game development using Unity. This lead to me creating a game in 2019 called "United Squares". It's a very simple 2D-Game where you have to fight an enemy who get's increasingly more powerful. At you disposal you have two types of weapons, a normal "gun" and a grenade and you can also build to protect yourself. The goal is to survive the longest and collect as many points as possible. The music was created by a good friend of mine that at that time was into music production. You can find him here.
    I currently don't have any downloads available, but you can enjoy a primitive (i.e. the kind that you would expect from a young teen) trailer I created back then on youtube.
