United Squares

In 2018 I got into game development using Unity. This lead to me creating a game called "United Squares" about a year later. It's a very simple 2D-Game where you have to fight an enemy who get's increasingly more powerful. At you disposal you have two types of weapons, a normal "gun" and a grenade and you can also build to protect yourself. The goal is to survive the longest and collect as many points as possible. The music was created by a good friend of mine that at that time was into music production. You can find him here.
